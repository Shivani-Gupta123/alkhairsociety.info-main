What is Islamic Banking?

Islamic banking, also known as Sharia-compliant banking, adheres to the principles of Islamic law (Sharia). These principles prohibit the collection and payment of interest (riba), promote risk-sharing, and ensure that all transactions are based on tangible assets or services. Al Khair Islamic Bank is dedicated to upholding these values, ensuring that your financial dealings are ethical and compliant with Islamic teachings.
