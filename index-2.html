Eligibility Criteria for Al Khair Bank Loans

To apply for a loan from Al Khair Bank, you must meet certain eligibility criteria. While the specific requirements may vary depending on the loan you’re interested in, here are some general guidelines:

Age: You must be between 21 and 60 years old at the time of applying for the loan.
Income: You must have a steady source of income, either from employment or self-employment, to demonstrate your ability to repay the loan.
Credit History: Al Khair Bank will evaluate your credit history and credit score to assess your creditworthiness.
Residency: You must be a resident of India and provide proof of your residential address.
